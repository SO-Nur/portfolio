WHO AM I ?

Hello There!

I'm Nur. A Lyon-based web developer and graphic designer with a passion for digital creation. I love incorporating my illustrative skills to add a unique flair to every project. I'm a firm believer in the synergy between design and development to deliver exceptional user experiences. My goal is to create digital products that are as visually stunning as they are user-friendly.

I'm mostly self-taught and I've always loved learning. When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me crafting something, whether it's sewing, building, drawing, or cooking.

As a curious and adaptable individual, I am passionate about continuous learning and personal growth. I thrive in collaborative environments and am equally comfortable working independently.
